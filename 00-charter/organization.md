# Organization Charter

> Knowledge Gate is not a heavy mission-driven institution.
> It is a community of people who believe knowledge should flow freely,
> doing one work-of-a-lifetime in a light, joyful, exploratory way.

---

## What Knowledge Gate is

Knowledge Gate (知识之门) is an **independent, protocol-based organization** dedicated to making high-quality learning content openly accessible. It is operated by contributors under a public charter, not by a single owner.

The founder participates inside Knowledge Gate as a contributor only. The founder's personal education businesses remain entirely separate and are not bound by, nor entitled to, Knowledge Gate's resources. This boundary protects both the founder's commercial freedom and the platform's public-good integrity.

---

## Things we believe

1. **Knowledge does not belong to anyone.** It belongs to everyone who needs it.
2. **The gate stays open.** This is the one non-negotiable condition.
3. **Each person earns what they should — no less, no more.**
4. **Protocol is bigger than the author**, including whoever wrote this charter.
5. **Exploration matters more than being right.** We allow trying, we welcome challenges.
6. **Light is not careless. Fun is not unserious.** We can be both at once.

---

## Things we explicitly reject

- Concentration of power in any single individual, including the founder.
- Closed-source decisions about how content is licensed or governed.
- Treating contributors as cheap labor.
- Hiding finances, hiding decisions, hiding mistakes.
- Using "the mission" to silence disagreement.

---

## Founder / commercial-business separation

| Aspect | Knowledge Gate | Founder's commercial business |
|---|---|---|
| Ownership | Collective, governed by protocol | Founder, governed by founder |
| Revenue | Public-interest pool, distributed per protocol | Private |
| IP | CC0 / open licenses by default | Founder's choice |
| Decisions | Charter + committee + community | Founder alone |
| Use of the other's resources | Forbidden | Forbidden |

The founder may *donate* time, content, or money into Knowledge Gate at any time. Knowledge Gate may *not* grant any preferential treatment back to the founder's commercial business.

---

## How this charter changes

This document is part of `knowledge-gate-spec`. Any change requires:
1. A pull request opened in this repository.
2. Discussion period of at least 7 days.
3. Approval by the governance committee per `governance.md`.
4. A corresponding entry in `CHANGELOG.md`.

Until a governance committee exists, the founder acts as interim steward but commits in advance to abide by every clause above, including the right of any future committee to overturn the founder's decisions.
